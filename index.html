Introducing
breathtaker1

play your computer keyboard like a wind instrument

breathtaker1 is a specialized plugin that maps your computer keyboard to saxophone fingerings, allowing wind players to control MIDI synthesizers with familiar techniques.

try for free learn more

control your synths with your sax brain

The breathtaker1 turns your computer keyboard into a playable MIDI controller using saxophone fingerings. No breath required — notes are triggered by keyboard presses. The transition from saxophone to bt1 is seamless, allowing wind players to immediately apply their existing skills.

It's also an interesting way for non-saxophonists to explore a monophonic instrument. The saxophone fingering system is logical, with note fingerings evenly split: half controlled by the left hand, half by the right hand.

Half the battle of playing saxophone is tone production, but when we strip that away and focus only on fingerings, how much easier will it be?
