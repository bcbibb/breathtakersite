About Breathtaker

Our Story

Breathtaker was born from a simple frustration: wind instrumentalists had no intuitive way to control synthesizers. Traditional MIDI controllers are designed with pianists in mind, leaving wind players to adapt their technique to an unfamiliar interface.

Our team of musicians and developers spent three years crafting a solution that allows saxophone, flute, clarinet, and other wind players to use their existing skills to control digital instruments naturally.

What began as a passion project has evolved into a powerful tool used by musicians around the world to bring the expressive potential of wind instruments into the digital realm.

The Technology

Breathtaker uses advanced signal processing to translate keyboard input into expressive wind controller data. Our proprietary algorithms analyze your playing style to create natural dynamics, articulations, and expression.

Unlike traditional MIDI controllers, Breathtaker responds to the nuances of wind playing techniques, allowing for bends, falls, growls, and other expressions that are essential to wind instrument performance.

By mapping familiar fingering patterns to your computer keyboard, we've eliminated the steep learning curve typically associated with electronic wind instruments, making digital music production accessible to acoustic players.
