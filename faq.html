Frequently Asked Questions

What is Breathtaker?

Breathtaker is a software plugin that allows wind instrumentalists to use their computer keyboard as an expressive wind controller. It translates key presses into MIDI data that reflects the articulation, dynamics, and expression of wind instruments.

Do I need special hardware to use Breathtaker?

No! That's the beauty of Breathtaker. It works with your standard computer keyboard. No additional hardware is required, though it can be enhanced with MIDI controllers if desired.

Will Breathtaker work with my DAW?

Breathtaker is compatible with all major DAWs including Logic Pro, Ableton Live, FL Studio, Pro Tools, Cubase, Reaper, and Studio One. It supports VST, AU, and AAX plugin formats.

I'm not a wind player. Can I still use Breathtaker?

Absolutely! While Breathtaker was designed with wind players in mind, anyone can use it to add more expressive control to their digital music production. The interface is intuitive enough for beginners but deep enough for professionals.

How does the licensing work?

Breathtaker licenses are perpetual - you pay once and own that version forever. The Standard version includes free updates for one year, while the Pro version includes lifetime updates. Licenses can be installed on up to two computers simultaneously.
